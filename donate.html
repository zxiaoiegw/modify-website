Donation

Your donations are tax-deductible as the Filipino Association of Greater Kansas. (FAGK) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Your contributions will ensure FAGK can continue to provide high-quality services and programs, acting out its mission to advance the well-being of Asian Americans. We need you! There are THREE ways to donate.

  1. Donate by PayPal
  2. Donate by check. If so, you can send your check to:

    Filipino Cultural Center
    Attn: Donation
    9810 W 79th St
    Overland Park, KS 66204
    (Memo: “Your Name” Donation)

  3. Donate in person

    We offer the option to visit our center for in-person donations via cash or check. Our dedicated staff will be available to assist and guide you through the donation process.
