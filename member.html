Membership

Membership is open to all interested individuals. Your membership dues support Filipino Association of Greater Kansas City.

Membership Rates:

  • Individual - $30.00 yearly
  • Family - $50.00 yearly (includes spouse and children below 18 years)
  • Individual, Senior - $20.00 yearly
  • Couple, Senior - $30.00 yearly
  • Patron - $50.00 yearly
  • Sponsor - $100.00 yearly
  • Benefactor - $250.00 yearly
  • Grand Benefactor - $500.00 yearly

Membership Benefits:

  • Discounts on select FAGKS events and programs
  • FAGKS e-newsletter
  • Free notary public service
  • Discounts to attend annual Gala

Please feel free to download the FAGKS Membership Application Form.
